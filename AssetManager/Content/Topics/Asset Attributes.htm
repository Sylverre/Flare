Asset Attributes

Asset attributes define fields to hold specific pieces of information about assets. Administrators may define as many asset attributes as needed to properly represent the various assets to be managed. supports a variety of different types of attributes. 

There are three main categories of Attributes: Asset Attributes, Status System Attributes, and Calculated Asset Attributes. The second two “types” of Attributes are the Status Attribute and the Calculated Asset Attribute.  The first is a group of pre-defined attributes that cannot be changed.  The second, Calculated Asset Attributes, are created and defined with formulas to create dynamic alerting and reporting features within and are a key component to Summary Assets.  The two special classes of attributes, as well as Summary Assets, are covered in separate sections of this document.

The first type of attribute is the main type of Asset Attribute.  These can be created and assigned to Asset Types.
