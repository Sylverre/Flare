Measuring RTI

  • The following requirements are necessary to calculate and monitor RTI:
  • Rack level instrumentation (sensors)
    • Optimal: 3 temperature sensors (top, mid, bottom) front & rear of every rack
    • Minimum: 3 temperature sensors (top, mid, bottom) front & rear of every 3rd rack
  • CRAC level instrumentation (sensors)
    • One temperature sensor on the CRAC supply
    • One (or more) temperature sensors on the CRAC return
  • The typical CRAC volumetric air flow rate (static value)
  • A software monitoring system (’s ) to collect and store sensor temperature readings and then to use them to compute RTI value.
