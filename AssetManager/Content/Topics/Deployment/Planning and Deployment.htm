Discovery, Testing, and Roll-Out

The main steps to take for deploying or any data center tracking and monitoring solution are below and can be summarized as Assessment, Testing, and Deployment:

  • Discovery (Assessment): information-gathering phase to prepare for the pilot and the subsequent full roll-out
  • Pilot Implementation (Testing): phase where a representative sample of equipment is used to demonstrate that a proof-of-concept solution is viable
  • Roll-out Implementation (Deployment): phase where equipment is staged and deployed to implement the solution

Discovery may have been completed before or after hardware and software delivery.
